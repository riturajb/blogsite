Pen & Flute

This is a celebration of world of poetry and music in the Indian subcontinent.
The focus in this blog is mainly on (but not limited to) :
- Poetry (of all forms in Urdu, English, Hindi, and other beautiful languages of Indian Subcontinent.
- Music of all forms but mainly Classical & Folk in the Indian subcontinent.


Note: Some of the posts will have subtle socio-political overtones which is inevitable given that might of the "pen" is involved. However the focus will not be on discussing the socio-political matters but on the artistic value of the poetry/music.
